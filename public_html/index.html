We look forward to getting to know your child! Our program addresses each child individually. Our mission is to build confidence and love of learning, giving students a solid foundation for a healthy and happy life.

Our teachers and staff are dedicated to helping each child discover their unique potential, academically, socially, and interpersonally. We invite you to learn more about us and to visit our center.

The Creative curriculum for Toddlers and preschool translates research and theory from the field of early childhood education into a practical, easy-to-understand approach to working with children and their families. It is a comprehensive curriculum with a clear organizational structure and a particular focus on routines and experiences.
